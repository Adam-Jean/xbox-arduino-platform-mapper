## ðŸŽ® Control Techniques for Marble Balancing

### The "Nudge" Technique
1. Marble rolling left (unwanted)
2. Tap stick right briefly
3. Platform tilts slightly right
4. Marble slows down
5. Return stick to center
6. Platform holds new angle

### The "Follow" Technique
1. Want marble to roll right
2. Hold stick right gently
3. Platform gradually tilts right
4. Marble starts rolling
5. Ease stick back to slow it down
6. Center stick when marble is where you want it

### The "Catch" Technique
1. Marble rolling fast to the right
2. Push stick right (same direction!)
3. Platform tilts right to "catch" the marble
4. Marble slows down as platform meets it
5. Ease stick back to center
6. Platform levels out with marble controlled
